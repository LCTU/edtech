Introduction

This learning object is intended for intermediate learners of Chinese who want to improve their readings skills. It is based on a short text that can be exploited in a number of different ways. In the Preparation section you will find a range of preparatory activities to help you read the text, including some background information, an introduction to the vocabulary and some general advice about different reading strategies. Depending on your needs and skills level you might try any or all off the approaches. Alternatively, you could go straight to the text and only come back to the preparatory section if you need additional help. The Text section allows you to explore the text in different ways. You can choose to see individual words highlighted, pick out proper names or look up key vocabulary items. You can also focus on the syntactic structures appearing in the text. The final section Follow up reviews some key items in greater depths to help you improve your ability to read authentic Chinese materials.

The text of this learning object talks about auspicious pattern in Chinese culture.
